# Community Development: Empowering Surrounding Communities (EN)

## Project Overview
This project aims to strengthen the relationship between Petrobras and the communities impacted by its operations. Our approach involves the collection and analysis of data to support decision-making and ensure transparency through information sharing.

An intuitive and interactive website will be created to serve this purpose, offering features for registration, data collection, and communication.

---

## üõ† Essential Features

### **1. Intuitive Design**
- **Objective:** Ensure users can navigate the website easily and efficiently, without barriers.
- **Features:** 
  - Responsive layout that adapts to different devices (desktop, tablet, smartphone).
  - Clear and simple navigation with well-organized menus and easily accessible sections.
  - Clean and attractive visual interface with colors and typography that promote readability and interaction.

### **2. Presentation Area**
- **Objective:** Provide clear information about the project, its goals, and its impact.
- **Features:** 
  - Sections about the project mission, interaction objectives with communities, and details on how data is collected and used.
  - Visualization of information with charts, maps, and other resources to explain the project in an accessible way.

### **3. Management Area**
- **Objective:** Provide tools for project administrators to manage interactions, data, and processes.
- **Features:**
  - Management interface with control panels, activity reports, interaction status, and data filtering.
  - Restricted access for administrators and field agents to ensure data confidentiality and security.

### **4. Data Integration**
- **Objective:** Consolidate data from various sources for analysis and informed decision-making.
- **Features:**
  - Integration with internal and external systems for efficient data import and export.
  - Use of structured databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL) to store and manipulate data at scale.
  - Real-time processing and dynamic report generation.

### **5. Registration and Login Area**
- **Objective:** Ensure users can register, log in, and access their information securely.
- **Features:**
  - Authentication system with login via trusted platforms like Gov.br, plus support for multi-factor authentication.
  - Option for new user registration, ensuring data security and a seamless login experience.

### **6. Profile Area**
- **Objective:** Allow users to personalize their information and track the progress of their interactions.
- **Features:**
  - User profiles with personal information, report history, and interaction status.
  - Ability to edit data, track the status of requests, and view feedback.

### **7. Reporting Area**
- **Objective:** Provide a channel for users to report concerns, suggestions, or experiences.
- **Features:**
  - Forms to submit reports with text, images, and videos.
  - Ability to categorize reports (e.g., environmental, social, infrastructure).
  - Track the status of each report, including the response from the responsible team.

### **8. Invalid Data Removal**
- **Objective:** Ensure the quality and reliability of collected data.
- **Features:**
  - Automatic algorithms to verify data validity, identifying inconsistencies, duplicates, or out-of-range values.
  - Manual data review system by responsible agents, with the ability to cancel or correct invalid information.

### **9. Feedback Area**
- **Objective:** Collect and organize user feedback to improve the project.
- **Features:**
  - Structured feedback forms with open-ended questions and rating options.
  - Analytical reports on received feedback, with insights into user satisfaction and areas for improvement.

### **10. Chat Area**
- **Objective:** Facilitate direct and real-time communication between the community and the project team.
- **Features:**
  - Internal chat system for quick inquiries and urgent issues.
  - Integration with third-party chat apps (such as WhatsApp or Telegram) to extend the reach of communication.

### **11. Access via Gov.br App**
- **Objective:** Ensure that the registration and login process integrates with official government platforms.
- **Features:**
  - Integration with the Gov.br system, allowing users to log in and register using their existing credentials on the government platform.
  - Additional security through Gov.br‚Äôs authentication system, ensuring reliable access.

### **12. General Data**
- **Objective:** Provide context and relevant information for the analysis of collected data.
- **Features:**
  - Display of relevant demographic, geographic, and socio-economic data for the communities.
  - Information on the environmental and social impact of Petrobras' operations in affected areas, sourced from reliable organizations like IBGE.

### **13. Third-Party App Chat**
- **Objective:** Offer users an alternative communication method via popular messaging apps.
- **Features:**
  - Integration with communication platforms like WhatsApp, Telegram, or Messenger, allowing users to communicate with the support team or leave feedback via these apps.

### **14. AI FAQ (Virtual Assistant)**
- **Objective:** Provide automatic responses to frequently asked questions, optimizing user service and time.
- **Features:**
  - An AI-powered virtual assistant that automatically answers common questions, providing detailed information and redirecting more complex cases to administrators.

### **15. Research and Local Support - Field Agents**
- **Objective:** Empower field agents to collect data directly from communities and provide support to residents regarding the project.
- **Features:**
  - **Data Collection:** Field agents will visit communities to gather relevant information such as socio-economic, environmental data, and reports of impacts or improvements perceived in the affected areas.
  - **Local Support:** Agents will be responsible for providing information to residents about the project's progress, answering questions, and guiding them on how to participate in initiatives or access project benefits.
  - **Report Registration:** Agents will have tools to record residents' reports and feedback, with the ability to submit this information to a centralized platform to ensure local concerns are documented and analyzed.
  - **On-the-Ground Support:** Agents will also be available to assist residents with practical issues related to the project, such as registration in support programs or clarifications on using the online platform.

### **16. Integration with IBGE API**
- **Objective:** Integrate geographic and demographic data from Brazil to enrich the analysis and decisions of the project.
- **Features:**
  - Access to demographic, social, and economic data updated through the IBGE API.
  - Use of geographic information and census data to map affected areas and identify community profiles.
